Balsamic Chicken

Easy balsamic chicken. Once you have the prep down this recipe should only take about 30mins. I usually pair it with green beans or asparagus.

Ingredients

Marinade
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoon balsamic glaze, store-bought.
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 lemon, or just juice to taste
  • 4 to 5 garlic cloves, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sweet paprika
Chicken
  • 8 boneless chicken thighs (I personally like to use tenderloin)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix together olive oil, balsamic glaze, tomato paste, honey and lemon juice.
  2. Add the garlic cloves, thyme, oregano, and paprika. Mix again.
  3. Add the chicken to the mixing bowl and make sure all the chicken is coated. Let it sit for at least a few minutes (A couple hours if you have time to prep ahead of time).
  4. Heat oven to 425 degrees F and adjust rack to the middle.
  5. Cook chicken in an oven safe pan for about 25 to 30 minutes. You want the internal temperature to be at least 165 degrees F.
